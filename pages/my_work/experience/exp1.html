Technical Support

Haverhill High School iSchool | Haverhill, MA

September 2013 - May 2014 { 9 months }

This experience was half class and half job. I interviewed for the dual opportunity to learn and to teach others during my senior year, and was one of the few people picked to ba a part of the tech support team. My time at the iSchool ended when I graduated High School.

At this job, I provided technical support to customers (both students and teachers at the school) by identifying problems, researching solutions, and guiding the customer through corrective steps, both in person and over the phone. In addition to this, I also helped to manage, issue, and maintain the carts full of iPads and laptops that we had at our school, making sure they were where they needed to go and that they were returned in good condition. I also performed inventory functions on newly purchased iPads and other tech-related materials.

There is so much that I learned during my time at the iSchool. I was taught how to make a succinct, visually pleasing, powerful powerpoint presentation, and how to make an even better prezi. How to use Google Forms and how to visualize the resulting data, and how to create custom styles in word processing applications were also some of the other things I learned. Another meaningful project was the creation of a few video tutorials, which gave me experience writing scripts, doing voice recordings and screen captures, and editing everything together into a successful video.
